Feeling in clothes

The feeling when is selected a clothe to dress

Happiness

By wearing a garment that makes us feel good, without feeling any insecurity or any other thought towards our outfit; it will keep something positive in our look by being happy with what we choose to wear, without rejecting our authenticity.

Sadness

Being sad when we choose what to wear creates a direct tunnel to insecurities without taking into account our opinion or authenticity, as only opinions that create bad feelings are present.

Despair

When we dress or don't know what to wear we feel desperation for not finding what we want or what prevents us from liking what we have on.

Dissatisfaction

When we no longer know what to wear we feel dissatisfaction when we feel that what we chose that could be a great look is not, or does not meet the expectation we wanted or expected.
